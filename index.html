Welcome to ACM Interview Club

We are the Interview Club at NCAT and are here to help you with everything coding related. From problem solving with programmining team to refining skills with interview prep, we do it all. Whether you are a beginner or an expert, this club has something for everyone.

Join

What We Do

Coding review and practice of important concepts (Strings, Arrays, Recursion, Trees, Graphs, etc.)

Interview prep (writing functions under pressure, big-O, how to carry yourself during a phone screen)

Resume reviews and how to apply for jobs

Building things for yourself (building a website, web apps, etc.)

Workshops/speakers on advanced topics (machine learning, data science, security, etc.)

Meetings

We meet every Thursday from 7:00pm - 8:00pm EST

* Note that both sessions are the same so you need only to attend one of them.
